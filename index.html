Meet Pat's Family!

It all started with Pat's visions to find the perfect treat for both people and cats. You see now, pat has a serious love for cats. Him and his family own 27 cats and 12 kittens. These treats are a hit with everyone! Deliciously created mostly sweet with a hint of fishy! Simply as Pat would describe as the puurrrrfect cookie! Pat and his family live in Seattle Washington where the first store was created back in 2010. Now this treat can be found all over the world! With every purchase, Pat and his family will donate 3% of profits to yougottobekittenme.com where we strive to find a home for every little furry friend. Please keep in touch and come back in December when Pat will be featuring his fishy christmas treats! A winter delight! You can find these started December 1st at every current location! We appreciate you and your love for cats too! Thank you for visiting....MEOW!
